# Sandyfeet Liwliwa Camp — Chatbot Knowledge Base

## About Sandyfeet
Sandyfeet is a beachfront resort and camp located in Liwliwa, San Felipe, Zambales, Philippines. It offers a relaxing coastal escape with rooms, day tours, camping, and various outdoor activities. The resort is known for its stunning waterfront views, pool area, bonfire setups, and laid-back beach vibe.

## Location
- **Address**: Liwliwa, San Felipe, Zambales, Philippines
- **Nearby landmarks**: Liwliwa Beach, Crystal Beach

## Room Types
Sandyfeet offers several room types for every kind of getaway:
- **Ground Floor Room** — Best for small groups or families. Air-conditioned, located on the ground floor for easy access, comes with basic amenities.
- **Group / Barkada Room** — Perfect for friend groups (barkada) and larger parties. Spacious layout, great for celebrations and group getaways.
- **Couple Room** — Intimate and cozy, designed for couples or solo travelers. Comfortable and private.
- **Tent / Camping** — Outdoor stay experience. Perfect for adventurous guests who enjoy camping, located within the resort grounds.
Visit the Rooms page on our website to see availability and current pricing for each room type.

## Booking Process
1. **Browse rooms or day tours** on the website
2. **Select your preferred dates** using the calendar (must book at least 2 days in advance)
3. **Choose room type and quantity** or set day tour guest count
4. **Fill in guest information** (name, email, contact number)
5. **Submit your reservation** — you will receive a booking reference number
6. **Upload payment proof** as instructed
7. **Track your reservation** using your reference number and email on the "Track Reservation" page

## Check-in and Check-out Times
- **Check-in**: 2:00 PM (14:00)
- **Check-out**: 12:00 PM (noon)
- Check-in and check-out times can be adjusted within allowed windows during booking

## Day Tour
- Day tours allow guests to enjoy resort facilities without an overnight stay
- Maximum capacity: 38 guests per day
- Guests can select a date and specify the number of adults and kids
- Day tour includes access to the pool, facilities, and resort grounds
- Must be booked at least 2 days in advance
- Pricing varies — check the day tour page for current rates

## Frequently Asked Questions

**Q: How far in advance do I need to book?**
A: Reservations must be made at least 2 days in advance.

**Q: Can I book multiple rooms at once?**
A: Yes! You can select multiple room types and quantities during the booking process.

**Q: What is included in the day tour?**
A: Day tour includes access to the pool, resort grounds, and available facilities. Specific activity inclusions may vary.

**Q: Is there a maximum number of guests?**
A: For day tours, the maximum is 38 guests per day. Room capacity varies by room type.

**Q: How do I know if my booking is confirmed?**
A: After submitting payment proof, the staff will verify and update your status. Track it on the "Track Reservation" page.

**Q: Can I bring pets?**
A: Please contact the resort directly for pet policies.

**Q: Is there Wi-Fi?**
A: Please contact the resort for current amenity availability.

**Q: What should I bring?**
A: We recommend bringing sunscreen, swimwear, extra clothes, and personal toiletries. The resort provides basic room amenities.
